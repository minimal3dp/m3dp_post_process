## III. Core Implementation Challenges for Integrity and Architecture

The success of your web application hinges on addressing the technical challenges of G-code modification, particularly ensuring the integrity of the output without destroying the model.

### A. G-Code Analysis and Manipulation

The foundation of your application must be robust G-code handling:

*   **G-code Interpretation:** The system must transform raw G-code text into a structured data model. This requires capability for **line-by-line analysis and restructuring**.
*   **Core Commands:** The optimization relies on dynamically rewriting specific commands, including kinematic controls like the **G1 feedrate** (for speed) and state controls like **M104/M140** (temperature) and **M221** (flow rate).
*   **Advanced Kinematic Optimization:** For quality and speed, advanced post-processing involves **Smoothing and Arc Fitting**. This technique converts the multitude of small linear segments (G1 commands) into smoother, continuous paths using arc commands (**G02/G03**) to minimize abrupt stops and acceleration/deceleration, resulting in faster travel and a smoother surface finish.

### B. Integrity Validation Complexity

The sources emphasize that guaranteeing geometric and kinematic safety is a critical challenge requiring high complexity implementation.
